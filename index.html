About

Crosstrain

Fight Club

Crosstrain is the brainchild of Siddharth Singh, India’s first BJJ Brown Belt and a Muay Thai Master. Founded in 2013, it has become a leading MMA academy, producing top fighters and promoting BJJ, Muay Thai, and self-defense in India. With a focus on elite training and empowerment, Crosstrain has shaped national and international champions while advancing combat sports culture in India.

our

Trainer

Siddharth Singh, India’s first Brown Belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and a Silver Mongkol ‘Master’ in Muay Thai, is a decorated fighter and entrepreneur. A former boxer from The Doon School, he earned an MSc from St. Andrews, Scotland, later working in London while mastering MMA, BJJ, Muay Thai, and Boxing. In 2013, he founded Crosstrain Fight Club, expanding it across North India, and launched ADCC India to promote grappling. With his expertise and vision, he has become a key figure in shaping India’s combat sports scene.
